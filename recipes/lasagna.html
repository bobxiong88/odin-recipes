Garfield's Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Jon's tears
  • Enriched uranium 235
  • 14 liters of 18.0M sodium hydroxide

Steps

  1. Add cheese
  2. Reduce Jon's tears with a low simmer until concentration of hydronium reaches 10^-5 due to the self-hydrogenation of water
  3. Mix sodium hydroxide with Jon's concentrated tears
  4. Sprinkle uranium-235 over lasagna
  5. Consume and ascend to Alma Torran
